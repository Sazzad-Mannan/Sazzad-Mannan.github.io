Website Design & Development

I specialize in website design and development, creating modern, responsive, and user-friendly websites. My focus is on clean layouts, smooth navigation, and optimized performance to deliver the best user experience. I work with front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, jQuery and frameworks, along with back-end tools such as PHP, Laravel, Codeigniter and MySQL.I also have expertise in moder javascript frameworks like React.js, Express.js, Next.js and MongoDB. I also develop custom WordPress themes and plugins, custom features, integrate APIs, and ensure mobile compatibility. Every project is built with attention to detail, scalability, and client needs in mind.

Web Domain and Hosting

I provide reliable domain and web hosting services with fast, secure, and scalable solutions. From domain registration to hosting setup, I ensure seamless performance, uptime, and support so your website stays accessible and runs smoothly.

API Development

I develop secure, scalable, and high-performance APIs that connect applications, websites, and third-party services. My APIs are designed with clean architecture, clear documentation, and high performance to ensure seamless data exchange and reliable functionality.

Android App Development

I develop Android applications with a focus on clean UI, smooth performance, and user-friendly experiences. My work includes custom video players, productivity apps, and utility apps tailored for global users. I ensure apps are optimized for different devices, responsive, and built with scalability in mind.
